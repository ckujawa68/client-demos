What makes us different

More Than a
Pizza Place

Procopio and Kristen Palazzolo started this restaurant in 2006 with one mission: bring the true taste of Italy to Vincennes. Every strand of pasta is imported directly from Italy. Every sauce is made in-house, every day.

Recognized on Indiana Foodways Alliance's Za Pizza Trail and Cultural Cuisine Trail — Procopio's is a statewide treasure rooted right here in Knox County.
